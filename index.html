The Vision

A Township Built
for Generations

Suvarnabhumi is a landmark residential township by Sandeep Dwellers Pvt. Ltd., offering 297 fully developed plots for bespoke villas and bungalows on Amravati Road — Nagpur's fastest-growing corridor.

With tree-lined boulevards, paver-block cement roads, 12m wide internal roads, and over sixteen world-class lifestyle amenities, this is an address that speaks before you do.
